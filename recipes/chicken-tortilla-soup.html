Chicken Tortilla Soup

Description

This is a recipe to make chicken tortilla soup.

Chicken tortilla soup is a simple to make, tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Bay leaves
  • Whole black peppercorns
  • Mexican oregano
  • Dried chilli pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine ingrediatents
  2. Cut tortilla strips
  3. Bake in oven
  4. Remove chicken
  5. Let chicken rest
  6. Blend everything together
