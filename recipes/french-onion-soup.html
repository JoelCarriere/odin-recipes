French onion soup

Description

This is a recipe to make french onion soup.

French onion soup is a simple to make, tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Beef broth
  • Thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • Provolon cheese
  • French bread

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine butter and olive oil, add in onions.
  3. Add beef broth and thyme.
  4. Let simmer.
  5. Cover with cheese and bake.
  6. Let the soup rest before serving.
